Our mission

Our mission is to accelerate the adoption of Electric Vehicles (EVs) there by making mobility more sustainable. By catalysisng EV adoption we endeavour reduce our nation's dependence on imported oil and make our air cleaner.

What we do

Ride It Private Limited is building the infrastructure needed to enable the EV Revolution in India. We are showing electric vehicles charging stations at various areas throughout the nation which would enable EV owners to travel long distances without the worrying about running out of charge, We are partnering with businesses that are frequented by EV owners such as restaurants, shopping malls and other commercial locations to install chargers in theiir car parks. As charging stations become mmore prevalent, car buyers will confidently choose EVs as concerns about chargers are addressed.
